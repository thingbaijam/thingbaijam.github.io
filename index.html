Welcome to my website!

It is designed to provide details of my research and related actitvities. I hope these would be interesting and useful to you.

My research interests cover a wide range of topics in Earthquake Seismology. These topics include Physics of Earthquake Source, Earthquake-source Inversions, Pseudo-dynamic Rupture Modeling, Ground-motion Simulations, Seismotectonics, Machine Learning Applications, and Seismic Hazard Analysis.
